Lasagna

Back

This is a lasagna. We gonna make it.

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Noodles

  • 1/2 pound lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta
  • 1 1/2 pound mozzarella
  • 1/2 large bell pepper diced
  • 1/4 pound parmesan

Instructions

  1. Simmer all sauce ingredients in sauce pan
  2. Boil the noodles
  3. When noodles are soft and sauce is simmered, assemble lasagna
  4. Throw in oven till cheese on top bubbles
